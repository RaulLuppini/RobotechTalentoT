CHARACTERS

Rick Hunter

Rick Hunter was an RDF (Robotech Defense Force) pilot and later, Supreme Admiral of the Robotech Expeditionary Force during the Pioneer Expedition.

Lynm Minmay

Lynn Mimmey was a Citizen of Macross Island and subsequently, the most famous singer. aboard the SDF-1 Macross during the First Robotech War.

Roy Fokker

Roy Fokker was a Robotech Defense Force Pilot.

Lisa Hayes

Lisa Hayes (full name Elizabeth Hayes) was the first officer of the SDF-1, serving under the command of Captain Henry Gloval and with her fellow bridge officers, her friend and confidant Claudia Grant, and the "Terrible Trio": Kim Young, Sammie Porter and Vanessa Leeds during the First Robotech War. He later became an admiral of the Robotech Expeditionary Force.

Henry Gloval

Commander of SDF-1. He is a veteran military officer, former captain of the Russian navy and one of the most experienced officers of Earth's forces. During the period of the reconstruction of Earth is promoted to admiral and is supreme commander of the forces of the Earth. He dies during the attack on the SDF-1, along with Claudia Grant, and the Terrible Trio

Claudia Grant

Claudia Grant was one of the SDF-1 Operators alongside Lisa Hayes, Sammie Porter, Kim Yeong and Vanessa Leeds.

Max Sterling

Maximilian Sterling was one of the first of the many young men of Macross City who enlisted in the Robotech Defense Force after the Zentraedi attack on Earth. Max He was preternaturally talented with the fighting method, which was almost perfect. When the lord of the war Khyron told pilot Miriya that there was a pilot from the Earth forces that could surpass even her, Miriya interpreted that it was Max, going so far to infiltrate the SDF-1 as a spy, so that he could finally best him in one competition.

Miriya Parina

Miriya Parina was a Zentraedi woman who served as an ace pilot within the Forces. Zentraedi Imperials before defecting to the Robotech Defense Force.

Ben Dixon

Ben Dixon was a pilot for the Robotech Defense Force during the First Robotech War, as well as well as a friend of Max Sterling. First Robotech War Ben, along with his best friend Max Sterling, became rookie wingmates under the command of newly promoted Lieutenant Rick Hunter, forming Vermillion Squadron with Roy Fokker watching them. While Max was focused, the burly Ben was quite jovial, sociable and comic. Ben was killed when the SDF-1's omnidirectional barrier system was overloaded, generating a explosion that devastated the Ontario sector, destroying its Veritech. (The Macross Saga: Point of Boiling)

Breetai

Breetai was a Zentraedi who became the commander of the Imperial Zentraedi Forces.

Khyron

Khyron is the commander of the 7th "Botturoi" division of the Zeltran army (the male division of the Zentraedi) commanding 15 ships, including his own, a Queadol Magdomilla, but under the command of Dolza.

El trio terrible

The terrible trio is a term used to describe the three official assists of the SDF-1 officer bridge: Sammie Porter, Vanessa Leeds, and Kim Young. In the English language known as "The Bridge Bunnies" The trio were killed in action during Khryon's suicide attack on the SDF-1 in episode 36. All three had the rank of lieutenant at the time of their deaths.
